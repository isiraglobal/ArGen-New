[SCORING_RUBRIC]

How we score. No black boxes.

Every challenge is scored across 4 dimensions. Each dimension is worth 25 points — for a total of 100 per challenge, per person.

C1

Clarity

0 – 25 pts

Is the output structured, legible, and immediately actionable? High scorers write like polished professionals. Low scorers need heavy editing.

C2

Constraint Application

0 – 25 pts

Did they follow the brief exactly? High scorers nail scope, format, and audience. Low scorers ignore half the requirements.

C3

Critical Thinking

0 – 25 pts

Does the response show original analysis — or just AI regurgitation? Top scorers challenge AI output and add their own judgment.

C4

Communication

0 – 25 pts

Is it audience-appropriate? Does it persuade? The best responses adapt tone and structure for the specific stakeholder.
