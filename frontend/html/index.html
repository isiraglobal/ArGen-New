5 Challenges. 4 Dimensions.
1 Clear Answer.

Why: Separates copy-pasters from strategic thinkers.
Tests: Data interpretation + AI-assisted insight extraction
Example: "Given this dataset of 6 months of sales data, use AI to identify the top 3 actionable insights and present them to a VP."
Why: Reveals who can turn data into decisions vs. who gets generic summaries.
Tests: Professional writing + AI editing workflows
Example: "Draft a client-facing project update email for a delayed deliverable. Use AI to refine tone and structure."
Why: Tests real-world communication under constraint.
Tests: Lateral thinking + iterative AI prompting
Example: "Your company needs to cut costs by 15% without layoffs. Use AI to generate and evaluate 5 options with trade-off analysis."
Why: Shows who uses AI as a thinking partner vs. an answer machine.
Tests: Implementation skill + AI code/automation leverage
Example: "Automate a weekly report that pulls from 3 data sources. Show your AI-assisted workflow and final output."
Why: Measures practical AI application, not theoretical knowledge.
