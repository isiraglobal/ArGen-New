Privacy Policy

1. Data Collection & Usage

We collect challenge responses strictly to generate evaluation reports for your organization. This includes text prompts, model outputs, and metadata related to the evaluation environment. All individual response data is anonymized after 90 days to be used securely in aggregate benchmarks for the AI performance index.

2. GDPR Compliance

We fully comply with GDPR standards for European customers. You have the right to access, rectify, or erase your personal data. You may request full data deletion at any time by contacting our support team at isiraglobal@gmail.com. We process data under the legal basis of "Contractual Necessity" to provide the evaluation services.

3. CCPA Compliance

California residents have the right to know what personal information is being collected, the right to delete that information, and the right to opt-out of the sale of personal information. Note: ArGen does not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Data Retention Policy

Raw prompt and output data are stored for 180 days for the purpose of historical comparison and report validation. After this period, data is either permanently deleted or fully anonymized for benchmarking purposes.

5. Third-Party Integrations

We use third-party services to facilitate our operations:

  • Stripe: For payment processing (we do not store credit card details).
  • Postmark/SendGrid: For system communications and report delivery.
  • OpenAI/Anthropic: For secondary validation of outputs (anonymized data only).

6. Cookie Policy

We use essential cookies to maintain session security and performance. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.
