The Perceptive Gap

In our survey, 85% of managers believed their teams were "highly proficient" in using LLMs for daily tasks. However, when put through the ArGen Challenge Framework, only 15% of those same teams could successfully apply complex constraints to a multi-step strategic problem.

Key Findings
