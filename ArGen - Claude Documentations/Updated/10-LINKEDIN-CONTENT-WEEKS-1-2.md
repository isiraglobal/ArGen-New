## WHAT NOT TO DO

### ❌ Avoid These Mistakes:

**1. Over-posting:**
- Don't post >1x per day
- LinkedIn algorithm penalizes spam
- Quality > Quantity

**2. Engagement bait:**
- Don't ask "Agree?" or "Thoughts?"
- Don't do polls about obvious things
- Don't use "tag someone who needs this"

**3. Premature selling:**
- Don't pitch in comments on other posts
- Don't DM people who didn't engage with you
- Don't ask for intros before providing value

**4. Fake metrics:**
- Don't buy followers
- Don't use engagement pods
- Don't inflate numbers in posts (people check)

**5. Inconsistency:**
- Don't post 5x one week, 0x the next
- Build rhythm, stick to schedule
- Friday updates are non-negotiable
